Working paper / under review

The Healthcare Fragmentation Index (HFI): Development and Initial Validation of a Reproducible Framework for Measuring Local Healthcare Environments

This foundational paper introduces HFI v1.1, applies it to New York City census tracts, and evaluates construct validity by linking hospital environments to CMS/HCAHPS communication outcomes.

  • Study area: New York City
  • Unit of index construction: census tract
  • Validation context: hospital communication quality

Future publication areas

Planned research areas include HFI v2 methodology, SPARCS-linked utilization outcomes, multi-city replication, and equity-focused extensions.
