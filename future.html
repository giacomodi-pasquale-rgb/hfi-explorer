Future Versions

HFI v1.1 is the foundational implementation. The website is structured to become the long-term public home for future versions, data releases, validation studies, and replication projects.

v1.1

Foundational spatial accessibility version

Reversed standardized provider time-weighted accessibility; New York City tract-level implementation; HCAHPS communication validation.

v2

Composite healthcare environment version

Candidate dimensions: specialty availability, provider capacity, language access, insurance networks, transit connectivity, and referral structure.

SPARCS

Administrative validation

Potential linkage to emergency department use, readmissions, length of stay, avoidable hospitalization, and follow-up-sensitive outcomes.

Replication

Multi-city HFI releases

Standardized construction across metropolitan areas to evaluate transportability and policy relevance.
